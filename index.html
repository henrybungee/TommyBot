Features

With over 15 commands, you can run your server like a pro. Here are a couple of features that TommmyBot is capable of.

Kick and Ban

Kick and Ban at will with TommyBot and keep your server tidy and clean.

Too much shitposting? Silence! Using TommyBot, you can mute someone for a specified amount of time whenever you want.

Fun
